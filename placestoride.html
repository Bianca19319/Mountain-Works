There are numerous places to ride, especially in New Zealand! If you are looking for places to ride in Auckland, well look no further as we have created descriptions, directions and trail recommendations for you.

List of some places to ride your bike
Places: Description
Woodhill Mountain Bike Park Woodhill Mountain Bike park is located North-West out of Auckland's North Shore area. The park has a variety of trails to ride and endure, reanging from a beginner difficulty, through to advanced. Entry into the park is $18 for adults and $8 for kids (ages 5-14).
Riverhead Bike Park/Hiking Park Riverhead Forest can be used for a variety of activities, such as mountain biking, motorcross and even hiking. The forest offers a variety of trails ranging from beginner through to advanced. Entry into the park is free.
Skyline Rotorua Bike Park Skyline Rotorua is a very well know mountian biking and tourist resort, that attracts throusands every year. The park is loctated in Red Woods Forest. Entry into the park is $45 for adults and $12 for kids (ages 4-14). The park has a wide reange of trail difficulty, from begginer trhough to expert.
