  • why ride?

Mountain Biking is important for exercising as it has been proven to be a good source of cardio exercise yet remains a safe and low-impact form of exercise for your joints. Mountain biking is a great opportunity to get amongst the outdoors and go exploring. It is proven that Mountain biking is better for your cardiovascular system, this is because the heart remains beating at a constant rate, making it better for the long-term as there is no additional pressure placed on it. If you are in the recovery process, for example recovering from a knee injury. Mountain biking is an excellent sport to participate in, as it can be done as a form of physiotherapy, in which helps to build the muscles surrounding the knee.
